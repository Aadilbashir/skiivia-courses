Why us?

Multidisciplinary tech-team with a can-do mentality.

Our courses cost less than teaching equivalent numbers of staff/students by running workshops or by building similar e-learning courses in-house. Online courses also save considerable amounts of administrative time and effort.

Whereas teaching a workshop or seminar can typically help 25 students at a time, our online courses open up audiences that can run into the thousands. Skiivia courses can be accessed remotely by those who might not have been able to attend workshops in person (e.g. part-time learners, distance learners).

Our courses work best if used to complement your existing provision. For example, they can be used to bring all learners up to the same standard and to deliver key basic knowledge and principles. Valuable face-to-face teaching time can then be used on richer and more thought-provoking content, such as problem-solving, case studies, practical applications and discussion.

Our courses are accessible 24/7 from any computer with an internet connection. This gives students the opportunity to study at a time, place and pace of their choosing to suit their learning needs and learning style.

In an increasingly competitive environment, our courses provide students with access to the very latest professional knowledge across a broad range of subject areas. You may not have in-house specialists available to cover all these areas; by using our courses, students will gain access to the knowledge of experts who are world class in their fields.
